Flipped Robotics Mini-Competition

Get ready for an exciting robotics competition! It's time to put your robots into action and score some points!

Updated Rules

Scoring:

  • Rings on Stakes: 5 points each
  • Top Ring on Stake: 10 points
  • Rings in Positive Corner Zone: 2 points each
  • Rings in Negative Corner Zone: -1 point each

Special Rules:

  • Removing opponent's rings from a zone is allowed and encouraged!
  • First to score gets an extra point per ring.
  • Bonus: If your robot can climb the ladder while holding a ring, you get 20 bonus points!

Prizes

Remember, there are great prizes up for grabs! The winning team gets the coveted "Competition Champion" trophy
