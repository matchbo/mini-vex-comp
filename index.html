Flipped Robotics Mini-Competition

Get ready for an exciting robotics competition! Put your robots into action and score points!

Updated Rules

Scoring:

  • Rings on Stakes: 5 points each
  • Top Ring on Stake: 10 points
  • Rings in Positive Corner: 2 points each
  • Rings in Negative Corner: -1 point each

Special Rules:

  • Removing opponent's rings is encouraged!
  • First to score gets an extra point per ring.
  • Climb ladder with a ring: 20 bonus points!

Prizes

  • VEX component vouchers
  • Pizza party for your team
  • "Competition Champion" trophy

Gear up, power on, and may the best bot win! Show your skills and claim victory!

Remember

  • Every challenge is a learning opportunity
  • Teamwork makes the dream work
  • Participating makes you all winners!
