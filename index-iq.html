Flipped Robotics Mini-Competition

Get ready for an exciting robotics competition! Put your robots into action and score points!

Updated Rules

Scoring:

  • Shoot a ball into the goal: 4 points each
  • Pass a ball to your partner: 4 points

Special Rules:

  • If the ball goes partly in the hole and then bounces out: 2 points

Prize

  • Custom-made "Competition Champion" plaque

Gear up, power on, and may the best bot win! Show your skills and claim victory!
